Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Gemini AI doctor accurate?

Our AI health assistant has been trained on extensive medical data and provides suggestions with a high degree of accuracy. However, it is designed to be a complement to, not a replacement for, professional medical advice.

Is my health information secure?

Yes, all your health information is encrypted and stored securely. We adhere to strict privacy protocols and comply with all healthcare data security regulations. Your data is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

Can I use the AI doctor without internet?

We offer a limited offline mode that provides basic diagnostic capabilities when internet connectivity is poor. However, for the most accurate and personalized recommendations, an internet connection is recommended.
